About Our Shelter


We provide education, advocacy, and shelter for animals in need. RoboGarden Pet Shelter was established as a non-profit organization in 1899 and exists to improve the lives of animals in our community by providing temporary shelter and appropriate new families for surrendered animals. Throughout the years, we have worked tirelessly to assist animals by providing them with a home until they can be adopted into a new family. We rely solely on individual and corporate donations in order to provide care for animals in our community.
